This privacy policy governs your use of the software application "Arth Sangrah" ("Application") for mobile devices.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The Application obtains the information you provide when you use the Application, such as transaction details (amount, category, date) that you manually enter. The use of the application is possible without providing any personally identifiable information.

Google User Data (Backup & Restore Feature)

For the optional Backup & Restore feature, the Application will request your explicit permission to access Google user data. The specific Google API Scope requested is:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file

Data Accessed:

The Application's use of this permission is limited to accessing its own configuration and database files stored in a private, hidden "appDataFolder" within your Google Drive. The Application does not request, and is not granted, access to any other files or folders in your Google Drive. It cannot see, read, or interact with your personal files.

Data Usage:

The Application uses this limited access for the sole purpose of uploading (for backup) and downloading (for restore) its own database file (arth_sangrah_db). This functionality is provided to you, the user, as a way to safeguard your data.

The Application's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

No. All financial data is stored locally on your device. Backup files are stored in your personal Google Drive, accessible only to you and this Application. We do not share your information with any third parties.
