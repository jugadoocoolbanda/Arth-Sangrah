Privacy Policy for Arth Sangrah

Last updated: 30/12/2025

Arth Sangrah is a personal finance application.

Data Storage

All of your financial data (transactions, budgets, etc.) is stored locally on your device in a private database. This data does not leave your device unless you choose to use the backup feature.

Backup & Restore Feature

For the Backup & Restore feature, the app will request your permission to access its own private folder within your Google Drive. The app uses this permission only to upload and download its database file for backup purposes.

The app cannot and will not access, view, or modify any other files or folders in your Google Drive.

Data Sharing

No personal data is collected, stored on external servers, or shared with any third parties.
