# Arth Sangrah: A Modern Personal Finance App for Android

An offline-first personal finance management application built with a 100% Kotlin and Jetpack Compose stack. Arth Sangrah helps you track your transactions, visualize your financial habits, and keep your data safe with secure cloud backup.

### Key Features:
*   **Transaction Tracking:** Easily add, edit, and view your income and expenses.
*   **Financial Visualization:** See your financial data visualized with powerful charts.
*   **Secure Cloud Backup:** Back up and restore your entire database to a private folder in your personal Google Drive.
*   **Biometric Security:** Protect your financial data with fingerprint or face authentication.
*   **Modern Tech Stack:** Built with Jetpack Compose, Coroutines, Hilt, and Room, showcasing a modern Android architecture.

---

## Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 30/12/2025**

Arth Sangrah is a personal finance application.

**Data Storage:** All of your financial data (transactions, budgets, etc.) is stored locally on your device in a private database. This data does not leave your device unless you choose to use the backup feature.

**Backup & Restore Feature:** For the Backup & Restore feature, the app will request your permission to access its own private folder within your Google Drive. The app uses this permission *only* to upload and download its database file for backup purposes.

The app cannot and will not access, view, or modify any other files or folders in your Google Drive.

**Data Sharing:** No personal data is collected, stored on external servers, or shared with any third parties.
